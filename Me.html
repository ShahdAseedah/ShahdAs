Brief Bio – Shahd Aseedah

An ambitious, dreamer, active youth worker , looking for new experiences, explore new worlds, courier for excellence, a believer in humanity, peace and love, and seeks to make a change in this world for a better future; This is me, a Palestinian woman, believed in herself and in the power of education, knowledge and open-mindedness.
My name is Shahd Aseedah, 26 years old; I have a bachelor degree of Computer Engineering and a Miner of Engineering Management. I’m currently work as MIS analyst for Middle East Investment Initiative (MEII), a non- profit organization, builds cooperative relationships with domestic and international destinations, leverages public and private resources to deliver and deploy finance programs that strike a balance between development and profits to stimulate sustainable economic development and investments in the Middle East and North Africa.
